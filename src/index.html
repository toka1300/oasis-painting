The number of coats needed is another factor; while some projects may only require a single coat, others, especially when changing between colours or dealing with specific surfaces, might need multiple layers. The type of paint chosen is another factor, with premium or specialty paints adding to the overall cost. Our detailed cost assessment takes all these factors into account, ensuring transparency and accuracy in our quotes. By evaluating the unique aspects of each project, we provide a customized estimate that shows the specific requirements and ensures the highest quality results for your residential painting investment.
