Transparent Value Pricing

Interior Painting Costs in Ottawa: What to Expect

Understanding the cost factors for interior painting helps Ottawa homeowners plan their projects more effectively. At Oasis Painting, we provide transparent pricing based on these and other factors.

  • Square Footage: The total paintable surface area serves as our baseline measurement
  • Project Scope: Painting ceilings, trim, doors, and baseboards increases the scope
  • Surface Condition: Walls requiring extensive repairs or preparation affect pricing
  • Paint Selection: Premium paints and specialty finishes influence material costs
  • Ceiling Height: Rooms with ceilings higher than the Ottawa standard (9 feet) require more time and materials
  • Colour Changes: Transitioning from dark to light colours often requires additional coats
  • Accessibility: Rooms with difficult access or many obstacles may require more time

Average Cost

At Oasis Painting, we understand that every interior painting project is unique. The cost of your painting service depends on the factors above along with many others. While most Ottawa interior painting projects typically range between $3.00-$6.00 per square foot, your final quote will be tailored to your specific requirements.

  • Our experienced team of interior painters in Ottawa ensures that walls are properly cleaned, patched and primed before any paint is applied. This ensures the best look possible when we are finished! Similarly, we will clean off all baseboards and trim before painting to get the best and longest-lasting results we can!

  • It is very common to paint over old paint, but a few precautions must be taken. We make sure that any surface being painted over is clean, without any grease/dirt spots that will affect the application of the new paint. Our next step is to ensure there is no chipped or peeling paint- if there is, we will sand the area and apply primer. Then we will patch any holes, or dents in the walls, sand them, and prime them as well. Finally, we are ready to apply the new paint. For any interior painting job for your Ottawa property, you can be sure that we will ensure it is done right!

  • Choosing a paint colour truly is a matter of personal preference but there are a few important things to consider. It's important to think about natural lighting and how a colour will bring out or take away from the lighting in the space. For example, some rooms with lots of light can look great with dark-coloured walls, while rooms with less light can look much darker when you change the walls to a darker colour. If you'd like to experiment with different colours in your space, you can try the paint visualizer from Dulux on our webpage!
