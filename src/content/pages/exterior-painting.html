Transparent Value Pricing

Exterior Painting Costs in Ottawa: What to Expect

Understanding the cost factors for exterior painting helps Ottawa homeowners plan their projects more effectively. At Oasis Painting, we provide transparent pricing based on these and other factors.

  • Square Footage: The total paintable surface area serves as our baseline measurement
  • Project Scope: Painting ceilings, trim, doors, and baseboards increases the scope
  • Surface Condition: Exterior surfaces get beat up from our harsh weather in Ottawa and can need lots of preparation work before painting, depending on their age
  • Paint Application: Multiple coats or primer requirements affect overall project duration
  • Accessibility Challenges: Work at heights requiring ladders or scaffolding increases cost per square foot
  • Paint Quality: Premium paints and specialty products like oil stains or rustproof metal paints influence total cost
