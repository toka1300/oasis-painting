Born & Raised
Our Roots

Local Ottawa Painting Experts Serving the National Capital Region

Oasis Painting's roots are local, through and through. Our founders Matt and Will grew up and have lived their entire lives in the Ottawa area, in North Grenville. This is why we bring our best to every project, striving to rise to the top of the Ottawa painting industry.

Since our inception, our commitment to customer service, knowledge of best products and best practices, and our detail-oriented approach have made Oasis Painting a respected and trusted name in Ottawa. Growing up in the area, we understand the painting needs of houses that endure Canadian winters and can recommend the proper process and paint products for maximum durability.

We are proud to serve the city of Ottawa and surrounding areas including Kanata, Orléans, Kemptville, and Brockville.
